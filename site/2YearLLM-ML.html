LLM/ML

Dr. Ambedkar Global Law Institute, Tirupati offers admissions to the aforesaid courses. The terms and conditions stipulated by Convener LAWCET, SV University, Tirupati apply as per the eligibility criteria specified below:

2 Year LLM/ML - Legum Magister/Master of Laws - Branch II - Criminal Law

Dr. Ambedkar Global Law Institute, Tirupati offers admissions to the aforesaid courses. The terms and conditions stipulated by Convener LAWCET, SV University, Tirupati apply as per the eligibility criteria specified below:

  • A pass grade in LAWCET
  • Pass mark is a minimum of 45% (~44.5%).
  • SC/ST Candidates have a 5% relaxation in qualifying marks
  • A pass in qualifying examination - degree for 3 Year LLB, Intermediate/+2 for 5 Year Integrated Courses
  • A candidate with 45% or more in BEd or other PG courses is also eligible to seek admission in LLB even if he/she secures less than 45% at degree level
